BoS Compass turns BoS into a personal plan. It helps you pick the right sessions, start fun conversations, and leave with follow‑ups that actually happen.

What it does (simple + useful)
Agenda that respects attention

“Must-see / nice-to-see / skip”, buffer time, and a single “what’s next?” screen with directions.

Networking that feels human

Openers + follow-ups + gentle reminders of what you care about. Also: an “escape hatch” button for awkward chats.

Notes → next actions

Talk notes that become follow-ups: who, what you promised, and a tiny next step you’ll actually do.

Conversation starters (fun, not cringe)
Choose your flavour
Use it like a spell: opener → follow-up → one real example. Then you’re in a real conversation.
