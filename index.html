A companion app concept

Make BoS Cambridge feel smaller. Plan faster, meet better, follow up.

BoS Compass is a lightweight conference navigator: it turns a busy schedule into a personal plan, helps you start conversations that don’t feel awkward, and captures the tiny next actions that make conferences worth it.

Offline-friendly Privacy-first notes Fast “what next?” Founder-to-founder tone

1) Decide what matters

Pick a focus (pricing, retention, AI, sales) and Compass keeps your day aligned — not just fully booked.

2) Navigate without friction

One screen answers: Where am I going next? Why? What question will I ask?

3) Meet people better

Conversation prompts with a founder vibe — smart, warm, and non‑cringe. Plus follow‑up templates.

4) Leave with actions

Turn talk notes into next steps. Make it obvious who to message, when, and what you promised.
